Multipurpose Hall

The Multipurpose Hall is a dynamic and versatile space that serves as a central hub for academic, cultural, and community activities within the school.

  • Guest Lectures & Panel Discussions: Equipped with stage, podium, and audio-visual systems for interactive sessions.
  • Debates & Elocutions: Designed with proper acoustics and focused seating arrangements.
  • Quizzes & Recitations: Flexible arrangements for team events and performances.
  • Workshops & Presentations: Movable seating for collaborative and interactive learning.
  • Meetings & Orientation: Adaptable layout for formal and welcoming setups.
  • Cultural & Other Events: Suitable for exhibitions, performances, screenings, and gatherings.

This hall fosters creativity, communication, leadership, and community engagement, making it one of the most vibrant spaces on campus.
