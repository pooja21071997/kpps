Sports Facilities

Sports play a vital role in the holistic development of students, offering far more than just physical benefits. Recognizing this, our school is committed to promoting sports as an integral part of education, understanding its direct connection to maintaining a healthy body and mind. Engaging in sports activities keeps students physically fit, active, and energized. It also develops their mental agility and emotional resilience, fostering a balance between physical exertion and mental well-being.

Our school is well-equipped to nurture this aspect of student development, boasting a range of sports facilities. There is a spacious football ground where students can engage in team play, learning the values of cooperation, strategy, and perseverance. Additionally, there are two volleyball courts and two kho-kho grounds, which not only promote physical fitness but also enhance skills such as speed, agility, and teamwork. The cricket ground serves as a venue for learning precision, patience, and focus, while the multipurpose basketball court provides a space for developing coordination, strategic thinking, and quick decision-making.

These sports facilities are not just physical spaces; they are arenas where students learn life lessons such as teamwork, leadership, discipline, and the importance of hard work. Participating in sports activities helps students develop a sense of camaraderie and sportsmanship, teaching them to handle both victory and defeat with grace. Moreover, regular physical activity has been shown to improve cognitive function, which positively impacts academic performance.
