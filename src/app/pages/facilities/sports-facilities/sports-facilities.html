Sports Facilities

Sports play a vital role in the holistic development of students. Our school promotes physical education as an essential part of academic life, ensuring students remain physically active, mentally sharp, and emotionally balanced.

The campus features a spacious football ground, two volleyball courts, two kho-kho grounds, a cricket ground, and a multipurpose basketball court. These facilities encourage teamwork, discipline, leadership, and strategic thinking.

Beyond physical fitness, sports teach resilience, sportsmanship, and the ability to handle both success and setbacks gracefully. Our sports infrastructure supports the development of confident and well-rounded individuals.
