Counselling Facilities

The school has a dedicated Counselling Centre providing personal and group counselling to support students' academic, emotional, social, and behavioural development. The centre also offers career and college guidance.

Psychological Assessments Include:

  • IQ Test: Measures cognitive abilities.
  • Psychoprofile & Personality Test: For understanding behavioural traits.
  • Career Test: Helps identify suitable career paths.
  • Study Habit Test: Evaluates learning patterns.
  • EQ Assessment: Measures emotional intelligence.
  • 16 PF Test: Comprehensive personality analysis.

The centre also addresses personal issues, academic struggles, confidence building, and behavioural concerns. Experienced psychologists offer guidance every Tuesday and Friday within the school premises.
