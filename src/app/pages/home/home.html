About Us

Our School is Nursery to 10th standard, which began in June 2008. Vivekanand Pratishthan English Medium School is an initiative of the Vivekanand Pratishthan Foundation and aim to provide world-class education focusing on the holistic development of young learners. The school is a contemporary urban school and a part of Vivekanand Pratishthan.

Knowledge, Respect, Social Responsibility, Creativity and celebration man making education, learning and sharing continuously, responsibility, achieving Excellency, nationalism and patriotism, social, sensitivity, quality education, team work are the core values which the school decided to adopt as a part of its identity.

Subsections of Institute

Pre Primary School

Early childhood education for children aged 3-5, focusing on foundational skills, social development, and creative play.

Primary School

Caters to children aged 6-11, offering basic education in literacy, numeracy, and foundational academic and social skills.

Secondary School

Education for students aged 11-16, focusing on advanced academic subjects and preparing for higher education or vocational training.
