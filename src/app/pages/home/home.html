About Us

Our institution is a CBSE school located in a beautiful natural atmosphere, slightly away from the town. It is a prestigious ten year old section of the Vivekanand Pratishthan which fulfills the need of wider section of society. We are running the school in three different shifts with 1700 students and approximately 100 staff members. Every class has 3 sections with around 40 students. We provide well-equipped science labs and a rich library to nurture academic excellence.
