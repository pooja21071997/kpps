Vivekanand Pratishthan's Kashinath Palod Public School

Our institution is a CBSE school located in a beautiful natural atmosphere, slightly away from the town. It is a prestigious ten year old section of the Vivekanand Pratishthan which fulfills the need of wider section of society. We are running the school in three different shifts, the total no of students studying in the school 1700 with a staff of (teaching and not teaching) is approximately 100.

Every class is having 3 sections with a strength of approximately 40. We have well equipped science lab and library to cater the needs of students. Computer lab is fully equipped with higher and software's, were student learns uncommon skills which help them in their progress. Recently school has initiated a innovative Robotics lab were 45 students can work at a time with a modern workstations. They are not only learning the electrical, mechanical, or electronic principles of robotics but they are making approximately 17 working models which they will take away home. Such is the beauty of our institution.

Education is meant to make life better, help personal growth, enhance thinking abilities, and lead to good morals.

-Dr. Avinash Ramachandra Acharya

Subsections of Institute

Pre Primary School

Nurtures young children aged 3-5, focusing on early learning, social skills, and creative exploration in a playful environment.

Primary School

Children aged 6-11, offering basic education in literacy, numeracy, and foundational academic and social skills.

Secondary School

An educational stage following primary school, focusing on advanced learning for Class 5 to 10
