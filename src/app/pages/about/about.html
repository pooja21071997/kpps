Vivekanand Pratishthan's Kashinath Palod Public School

Our institution is a CBSE school located in a beautiful natural atmosphere, slightly away from the town. It is a prestigious ten year old section of the Vivekanand Pratishthan which fulfills the need of wider section of society. We are running the school in three different shifts, the total no of students studying in the school 1700 with a staff of (teaching and not teaching) is approximately 100.

Every class is having 3 sections with a strength of approximately 40. We have well equipped science lab and library to cater the needs of students. Computer lab is fully equipped with higher and software’s, were student learns uncommon skills which help them in their progress. Recently school has initiated a innovative Robotics lab were 45 students can work at a time with a modern workstations. They are not only learning the electrical, mechanical, or electronic principles of robotics but they are making approximately 17 working models which they will take away home. Such is the beauty of our institution.

Education is meant to make life better, help personal growth, enhance thinking abilities, and lead to good morals.

Principal's Message

Dear Parents, Students, and Well-wishers,
Greetings from Vivekanand Pratisthan’s Kashinath Palod Public School!
It gives me great pride to welcome you to this esteemed institution. At KPPS, we believe in holistic education that goes beyond textbooks and exams. Our aim is to shape confident, responsible, and future-ready individuals.
We focus on developing essential 21st-century skills such as critical thinking, creativity, problem-solving, and digital literacy—ensuring our students are well prepared for the rapidly evolving global world.
Our educational approach is aligned with the National Education Policy (NEP), promoting independent thinking, innovation, and a multidisciplinary learning experience.
Along with academic excellence, we instill strong Indian cultural values and encourage participation in sports and extracurricular activities. We believe that a balance of knowledge, character, and physical fitness leads to complete development.
As we move forward together, I request the support and involvement of all parents and well-wishers in shaping the bright future of our children. Let us work as a team to inspire our students to dream, achieve, and contribute positively to society.
Looking ahead to a wonderful journey of learning and growth!
